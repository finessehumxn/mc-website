Frequently Asked

Questions we
hear all the time.

What services does Millennials Creatives offer?
+
We offer eight core service areas: AI engineering and digital operations, brand strategy and identity design, 3D design and visual systems, institutional and operational consulting, healthcare and public health consulting, public health and safety systems, training and professional development, and marketing and content strategy. Each service is led by a credentialed co-founder.
Who typically hires Millennials Creatives?
+
We work with a wide range: startups and entrepreneurs building their brand from scratch, established companies ready for a rebrand, nonprofit organizations that need operational and digital support, higher education institutions, and government agencies procuring consulting services through Arizona's APP portal or SAM.gov. If you're serious about results, you're our kind of client.
How long does a typical project take?
+
Brand identity projects typically run 3–7 weeks depending on scope. Website and digital systems projects run 6–12 weeks. Consulting engagements are scoped per project. Government contracts follow the timelines set in the solicitation. We'll give you a specific timeline during our initial consultation — and we stick to it.
What makes MC different from other agencies?
+
Our four co-founders collectively hold credentials in AI engineering, registered nursing and public health (BSN, RN, PHN), nonprofit organizational leadership and an MBA, and public health and safety. That combination doesn't exist anywhere else in a firm our size. On every project, a co-founder leads — you're not passed to a junior team. And as a woman-owned, minority-founded firm, we bring an authentic commitment to equity that shapes every engagement.
Can you work with government and public sector clients?
+
Yes. We are registered federal vendors on SAM.gov (UEI: WBGAAWMD3YE5, CAGE: 18ZQ0) and registered in the Arizona Procurement Portal. We currently have active bids with multiple Arizona state agencies. We can provide W9, capability statements, and past performance documentation on request. Government procurement inquiries should contact us directly.
Do you work with clients outside Arizona?
+
Absolutely. We are remote-first by design and have worked with clients across the US. Our co-founders collaborate digitally and can provide on-site presence for government or institutional engagements when required. Geography is not a barrier.
What are your payment options?
+
We offer flexible payment structures: 50/50 split (half upfront, half at delivery), 3-part payment (30/35/35%), or a 4-month plan via AfterPay or Klarna with a 6% fee. Government and institutional clients are invoiced Net 30. We'll agree on the payment structure during your intake consultation.
How do I start a project?
+
Fill out our intake form below with details about your project, timeline, and budget. We review every submission within 48 hours and follow up to schedule a consultation call. If we're a strong fit, we'll send a proposal within 5 business days. No hard sells — just an honest conversation about what you need and whether we're the right team for it.
